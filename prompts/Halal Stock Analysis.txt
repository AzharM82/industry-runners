# Halal Stock Compliance Checker Prompt

Use this prompt to analyze any stock for Shariah compliance according to AAOIFI standards:

## Prompt Template:

**Analyze [STOCK TICKER: Company Name] for Islamic finance (halal) compliance using AAOIFI standards. Please verify the following criteria and provide specific calculations:**

### **REQUIRED ANALYSIS:**

**1. BUSINESS ACTIVITIES SCREENING (Qualitative)**
- What is the company's primary business/industry?
- Does the company engage in any prohibited (haram) activities?
- Prohibited sectors include: alcohol, gambling, pornography, conventional banking, insurance, pork products, tobacco, weapons, adult entertainment
- What percentage of revenue (if any) comes from questionable/prohibited sources?

**2. FINANCIAL RATIOS SCREENING (Quantitative)**

**A. Debt-to-Market Cap Ratio:**
- Find: Total interest-bearing debt (long-term + short-term debt)
- Find: Current market capitalization (stock price × shares outstanding)
- Calculate: (Total Debt ÷ Market Cap) × 100
- AAOIFI Threshold: Must be ≤ 30%
- Status: Pass/Fail

**B. Interest Income Ratio:**
- Find: Most recent quarterly interest income amount (from investments, bank deposits, etc.)
- Find: Current year revenue expectations (based on latest quarterly results and guidance)
- Calculate: (Latest Quarterly Interest Income × 4 ÷ Expected Annual Revenue) × 100
- AAOIFI Threshold: Must be ≤ 5%
- Status: Pass/Fail
- Note: Use most recent quarterly data and forward-looking revenue projections for current compliance assessment

**3. DATA SOURCES TO CHECK:**
- Latest 10-K annual report
- Recent quarterly earnings reports (10-Q)
- Current stock price and market data
- Income statement for interest income details
- Balance sheet for debt information

**4. VERIFICATION WITH ISLAMIC FINANCE PLATFORMS:**
- Check compliance status on: Zoya Finance, Islamicly, Musaffa, Muslim Xchange
- Note any discrepancies between platforms
- Provide screenshots or direct quotes from these platforms

**5. FINAL ASSESSMENT:**
- Overall Shariah Compliance Status: Halal/Haram
- Primary reasons for pass/fail
- Specific recommendations for Muslim investors
- If compliant but has impermissible income <5%, calculate dividend purification percentage

**6. INDUSTRY ALTERNATIVES:**
- Research 3 stocks from the same industry/sector as the analyzed company
- Provide: Company Name, Ticker Symbol, and 50-day Average Volume
- Note: These are for informational purposes only, not investment recommendations
